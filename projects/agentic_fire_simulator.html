Agentic Fire Simulator

Overview

The Agentic Fire Simulator is a computational model designed to simulate the spread and control of fire using agent-based techniques. It helps in understanding fire dynamics and testing various intervention strategies.

  • Python
  • Simulation
  • Agent-Based Modeling
  • Visualization

Features

  • Customizable environment and parameters
  • Visualization of fire spread
  • Agent-based control strategies
  • Performance analysis and reporting
